Dandeli Travel Diaries

Dandeli Jungle safari

Dandeli Jungle safari

Dandeli peacock

That was a amazing day.. Me n my friends planned to discover Dandeli which is 240 km from my place… around 6 hour drive. On that day.. we started our journey at 6 am. First one n half hour was quite routine as it’s on highway . @7.40am we crossed Belgaum n left NH4.. Then hilly landscapes started.. n the nature started showing mesmerizing views.. Light rain showers.. small hills.. lush greenery.. cool breezes.. soft music.. n best friend.. right combination for trip. I was enjoying n realized that this trip is going to be memorable…

Dandeli travel

Dandeli was about one hour now.. we took a stop for a breakfast…

After having a tasty idli sambar and coffee we moved to out destination.Roads was quite good and washed away with rains . Both side tall heightened tree jungle. At around 11.00 we reached our homestay in dandeli..

Travel stories of frisky girl Pooja patel;Adventures

When you reach dandeli first thing you notice roaring sound of magnificent Kali River..Kali River is center of attraction in dandeli.. it flows through amides jungle. Dandeli is famous for adventure activities like jungle safari,bird watching,river rafting and night Safari .We have only one day in hand. So we decided to go for river rafting.. n it was a lifetime experience.Rafting through white water of Kali River was an amazing experience.

Dandeli is heaven for nature photographer… You don’t need to set camera angles and all just lift camera and click you will get amazing landscape..Kali River kavla caves crocodile hunt dandkaranya anshi national park are the famous tourist spots.. you need at least three days to experience all In the evening we stayed at homestay near River edge.. owner arranged bon fire for relaxation.Next morning we started our return journey with sweet memories n promise that we will come again here for long stay...
